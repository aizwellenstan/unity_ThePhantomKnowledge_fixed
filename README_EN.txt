The Phantom Knowledge is published under the UCL. The data is compatible with Unity 2017.1.1f1 or later versions. This demo project works on the Windows Direct X11 and on the Mac Metal environment.

System requirements:

Windows: CPU 3.5Ghz(4 cores) RAM 16GB Geforce 960GTX or more.
Mac:     CPU 3.5Ghz(4 cores) RAM 16GB Radeon R9 M395 or more.

You may encounter some issues in other environments. If the project is playing too slowly in the Unity Editor, try closing the inspector window and the timeline window.
